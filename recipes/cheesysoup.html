Cheesy Soup with Bacon

Description

This is a deliciously creamy soup that is sure to please, especially during the winter months, when comfort food is calling.

With a cheesey potato taste, accented by scallions and bacon, this soup is a sure winner with everyone, from kids to adults. Easy to prepare, it will only take you a half hour to make this delicious soup, and leftovers (if there are any) will stay fresh in the fridge for up to two weeks. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of frozen hash browns
  • 2 cups cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 oz. cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions

How to Make

  1. Combine hash browns, bacon, onion, chicken broth, salt, pepper in a slow cooker. Stir well, and place lid on pot or slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low heat, until onion is soft, usually for 2 hours
  3. Mix in cheddar cheese and stir until melted and combined.
  4. Cover and cook for additional 30 minutes.
  5. Serve topped with green onions as garnish.
